TickTackToe

A game of TickTackToe with different types of opponents
Offense, Defense, Hybrid uses determined win conditions to decide next move, all of these where hard coded so where not scalable and had no lookahead. the hybrid was a mix of both Offense and Defense and plays similar to MinMax but is modular and only works for this game.
MinMax Uses Recursion to lookahead in the Game and determine the best move to make based on the returned score.
